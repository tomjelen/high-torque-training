Intro — what & why
▾

High-torque training is low cadence at high power. In an 8-week study, cyclists doing intervals at 50–70 RPM gained +8.7% VO2max / +8.1% MAP, vs +4.6% / +3.0% at freely chosen cadence >80 RPM.

→ Study (Hebisz & Hebisz, PLOS ONE 2024) · Podcast that inspired this

How this site is structured: start with the adaptation phase below, then use the collection weekly. 1–2 sessions/week, never back-to-back.

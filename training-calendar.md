# High Torque Training — Calendar

Track each session by replacing `[ ]` with `[x]`. Add notes in the Notes column — how your knees felt, whether you hit the cadence targets, anything worth remembering.

The calendar does not prescribe specific days of the week. Fit the sessions into your existing schedule. The only rules: no low-cadence sessions on back-to-back days, and always a rest or normal-cadence day between Phase 3 Day 1 and Day 2.

# About me — draft copy

Written during the intro panel redesign. Pulled from the panel for being too long there,
but could work on a dedicated about/bio page or similar.

---

I have training plans. I just don't follow them. A good challenge or a beautiful route
always beats a TSS target — and I'm fine with that. I ride to enjoy it.

I'm in my mid 40s[^weight], a natural min/maxer (coffee gear, work, games) who never applied
that instinct to structured training — until last summer in Andorra. Some pros passed me on
a climb grinding so slowly it looked wrong. I watched
[a podcast](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tcar3G2v73I), understood what they were doing,
and something clicked: one structured hour a week, high ROI, actual peer-reviewed studies
behind it.

[^weight]: And now getting fatter year by year — ~1kg of weight gain per year since 40.
Used to feel bad about it. Made my peace. I do worry about the projections for age 100.
